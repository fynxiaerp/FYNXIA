- computeIrrf(baseCalculo, brackets): ≤5000 → 0; 5000.01–7350 → base×0.275 − (978.62 − 0.133145×base); >7350 → base×0.275 − 908.73; integer-cent

- computeIss(valorServicos, aliquota): integer-cent (computeIss(1200,0.05)===60)

Reconciliation targets (16-RESEARCH.md §"Algoritmo de Conciliação"):
